Percentage Variation

How is this information useful?

The estimated splits are based on data from solves of top Big Cube solvers, and it is concluded that any variation less than 20% is not very significant. If you find there is a stage in your solve which is more than 20% higher than the suggested splits, this may be an area where you would want to work on.
